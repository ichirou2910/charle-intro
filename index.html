Statistics are attributes a character can have, which greatly affect the character's performance in battle.

Each character has 3 stats with unique base value and cannot be changed, only strengthened through levelling up and exceeding:

  • The Attack stat affects the character's Damage output, Armor gain or Heal value.
  • The HP stat shows the amount of damage the character can take before being downed.
  • The Resurrection stat shows the number of resurrection orbs it takes to bring the character back after being downed.
Formula
Statistics = B x 1.06L - 1 x RM x EM
Where:
  • B = Base stat of the character
  • L = Current level of the character
  • RM = Resonance multiplier
    • Tier N: RM = 0.9
    • Tier R: RM = 1
    • Tier SR: RM = 1.2
    • Tier SSR: RM = 1.35
  • EM = Extra exceed multiplier (see below)
